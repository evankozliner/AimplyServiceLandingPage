FAQ

How does Aimply charge?

Aimply bills per hour and on attributable growth or sales on a case-by-case basis. Low-skill services like video posting are around 15$ per hour. For any content we generate, we also charge based on growth and sales (in the 10-20% range) as well as an hourly base fee depending on the type of content being generated.

What is the full list of services Aimply provides?

1. Research assistance: This can either be through our automated tools, or from a dedicated virtual assistant with a bachelor's degree.

2. Video editing and posting: We can edit short or long form videos to add effects, B-roll, photos, etc. We also cut long form content to short form content for platforms like Tiktok. We're also happy to post videos onto platforms manually to avoid penalties for auto-posted content.

3. AI content generation: We can generate tweets, blog posts, ebooks, all in your voice and tone. All content is generated based off your already-existing content; e.g. we can generate tweets using what you've said in your Youtube videos. We do not try to create "novel" content with AI.

4. Community management: We can manage and set up Discord servers, Patreon accounts. We can act as moderators and handle responding to subscriber's questions. We'll remind your subscribers of your Q&As, keep your Discord "warm", and much more.

Why use Aimply over hiring virtual assistants from within my community?

Hiring within your community can be great, but oftentimes it leads to working with less experienced employees who need rehired or let go frequently due to low hours and lack of upward mobility. At Aimply we work to ensure even our most junior employees have experienced supervision and appropriate weekly hours; this increases quality and reduces churn.

What makes Aimply better than Upwork or similar contractor platforms?

Unlike platforms like Upwork, we can be hooked into all aspects of your content, from research to content production and community management. Because we have such a holistic view of your content and your community, we can produce higher quality work.

I'm nervous about the AI content - couldn't this damage my brand?

At Aimply, we treat AI content as a mass of unformed clay that needs molded by human beings, not a final product. 100% of Aimply's AI content is quality assure, edited by our team, and approved by the creators it is posted on behalf of, so you can be sure it won't cause your brand damage.

For AI content, how do you ensure the content is on-brand?

We fine-tune our models to your pre-existing content to ensure that whatever we generate has the correct style for your brand.
